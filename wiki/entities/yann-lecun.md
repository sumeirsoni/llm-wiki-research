# Yann LeCun

## Overview

VP and Chief AI Scientist at [[meta-fair|Meta FAIR]]. Turing Award laureate (2018). One of the founding figures of deep learning. Principal architect of the [[jepa|JEPA]] paradigm.

## Role in This Wiki

LeCun is a co-author on **4 of 7 papers** in this wiki — the highest overlap of any researcher:

- [[causal-jepa|Causal-JEPA]] — object-centric world model
- [[lejepa|LeJEPA]] — theoretical foundations of JEPA (with [[randall-balestriero|Balestriero]])
- [[leworldmodel|LeWorldModel]] — end-to-end JEPA world model
- [[v-jepa-2-1|V-JEPA 2.1]] — dense video self-supervised learning

## JEPA Vision

LeCun has publicly advocated for JEPA as a path toward autonomous machine intelligence, arguing that:
- Generative models waste capacity on predicting irrelevant details
- Contrastive methods are limited by the choice of augmentations
- JEPA learns at the "right level of abstraction" — in latent space

His research group has systematically explored JEPA across images (I-JEPA), video (V-JEPA), and world models, while also investing in theoretical foundations ([[lejepa|LeJEPA]]).
