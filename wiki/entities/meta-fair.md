# Meta FAIR

## Overview

Meta's Fundamental AI Research lab, led by [[yann-lecun|Yann LeCun]]. The primary institutional home of [[jepa|JEPA]] research.

## Papers in This Wiki

- [[causal-jepa|Causal-JEPA]] — object-centric JEPA world model
- [[lejepa|LeJEPA]] — theoretical foundations + SIGReg regularizer
- [[leworldmodel|LeWorldModel]] — end-to-end JEPA from pixels
- [[v-jepa-2-1|V-JEPA 2.1]] — dense video self-supervised learning (flagship)

**4 of 7 papers** originate from Meta FAIR, making it the dominant institution in this wiki.

## JEPA Roadmap

Meta FAIR has systematically developed the JEPA paradigm:
1. **I-JEPA** (2023) — original image JEPA
2. **V-JEPA** (2024) — extension to video
3. **V-JEPA 2** (2024) — scaled video JEPA
4. **[[v-jepa-2-1|V-JEPA 2.1]]** (2025) — dense features + deep self-supervision
5. **[[lejepa|LeJEPA]]** (2025) — theoretical foundations
6. **[[leworldmodel|LeWorldModel]]** (2025) — world model application
7. **[[causal-jepa|Causal-JEPA]]** (2025) — causal world model

## Contrast with Other Labs

- **Apple** ([[rethinking-jepa|SALT]]) — challenges EMA-based training, proposes frozen teacher
- **Stability AI / MIT** ([[self-flow|Self-Flow]]) — goes beyond joint-embedding to integrate generation + representation
- **Academic** ([[bootleg|Bootleg]]) — multi-layer distillation approach
